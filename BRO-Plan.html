Goals

The purpose of the Barrier Reef orchestra website is to spread awareness of its brand to a larger intended audience within the Queensland area and increase the amount of participating members and supporters for the Orchestra.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be determined by an increase in the amount of members which have signed up using the new site, including a measure of donations made through the website. The website will be considered successful if there is a 20% increase in membership, after advertisement of the new site.

Target Audience

The website will target a young-adult audience who have a love for, or desire to learn, music.

A design which resonates with the prestige of classical music, whilst containing a hint of modern design.
